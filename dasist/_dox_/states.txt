Исполнитель: Cancel, Save, Send
Подписатель: Accept, Reject
Бухгалтер: Оплачено, Reject

So, pages:
* list (/) - GET; ACL: assign|approve=user
* add (/c/) - GET/POST; ACL: assign, Cancel > list; save > view (Draft)
* view (/<id>/r/) - GET; ACL: assign|approv
* edit (/<id>/u/) - GET/POST; ACL: assign+draft;
* resume (/<id>/y|n/) - POST; assign/reject w/ option comment (POST)
